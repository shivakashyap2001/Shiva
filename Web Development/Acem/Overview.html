Overview

Allahabad College Of Engineering And Management is situated in Kushinagar in Uttar Pradesh state of India. Established in 2010, and it is affiliated to Maharishi University of Management and Technology. ACEM, Kushinagar offers 9 courses across 1 streams namely Engineering and across 1 degrees like Diploma.Hostel facility is available for its students. Additional campus facilities such as Canteen, Computer Lab, Library, Medical Facilities, Sports, Grounds, Trans., Workshops, Hostel are also there.
