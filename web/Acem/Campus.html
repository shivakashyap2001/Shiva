Campus

Library

The library offers students an excellent selection of books and magazines on all the branches ofengineering, basic sciences, professional communication and management. It has more than 30,000 books & audio-visual learning resources. The center is digitized with computerized Issue & Return of books, computer-assisted search of learning resources and Open-Access System. It has facility for Internet and Del-Net. Library subscribes to about 100 National & International Journals.

Computer Lab

The change in teaching from conventional to contemporary methods has made Computer an integral part of learning. The Computer Centre has latest configuration of PCs with Core2Duo & P4 processors and provides round-the clock 24x7 accessibility to internet so that the students can access the information about latest advancements taking place across the globe.MSDN alliance provides licensed software usage as a versatile package.Other software include JAVA DEVELOPMENT KIT & DBMS PACKAGE, together with other system & application Software.
